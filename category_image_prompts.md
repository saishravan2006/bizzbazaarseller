## Usage Instructions

4. Download and save as: `{category_code}.webp` (e.g., `P01.webp`)
5. Place in: `public/category-images/` folder

- **Format:** WebP (preferred) or PNG

- **Style:** Clean, professional, e-commerce ready
- **Background:** Pure white (#FFFFFF)
